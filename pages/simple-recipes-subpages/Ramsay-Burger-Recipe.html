Gordon Ramsey's All American Burger Recipe

Return to Simple-Recipes menu? Click here

Before we start with the process of actually making our delectable burger, we must first actually have our ingredients ready for the burger making process!

You will need:

  • A stove
  • A pan
  • A spoon
  • A lid for the pan
  • Canola Oil
  • butter
  • A fresh, raw burger patty
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter
  • Onions
  • Cheese
  • Burger buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Dijon Mustard (You can just use normal mustard if you don't have Dijon Mustard on hand)
  • Pickles

Directions for cooking your burger, meling cheese, and caramelizing your onions:

  1. Place your pan on your Stove
  2. Drizzle your pan with Canola oil
  3. Pick up your pan and move move it around to spread the canola oil throughout the pan
  4. Once the Canola oil has spread around your pan, place the pan on your stove
  5. Turn on your stove to about medium-high heat to get your pan nice and warm
  6. Place your burger patty in the pan, perferabbly at the center of the pan
  7. Season your burger patty with salt and pepper
  8. As the burger starts caramelizing, get your butter and cut off about half a table spoon of butter
  9. Turn down the heat of the stove and use the remaining heat in the pan to continue cooking the burger
  10. Tip the pan over to get the juices on one side, and use your spoon to scoop up the juices and pour them on the burger to give it more flavor
  11. Continue cooking the burger for about 2 minutes or until it has a good brownish texture
  12. Once the burger appears to have a good sear on the bottom flip it over and cook the other side for about another 2 minutes or until it has a good sear on the other side
  13. Add another half a table spoon of butter for more flavor
  14. Grab your onion and cut off about 3 slices
  15. Place your slices into the pan to caramelize the onions
  16. Add pepper onto your patty and onions
  17. Once the onions appear to be cooked well enough on one side, turn them over
  18. Grab a slice of cheese
  19. Season your burger with a bit of salt, then place your cheese on top of your patty
  20. Grab another slice of cheese
  21. Season your cheese on your patty with some salt, the place your other slice of cheese on top of the patty with cheese
  22. Season your patty with double cheese with a bit more salt and pepper
  23. To melt your cheese quickly grab your pan lid, turn off your heat and place the pan lid on top of your pan
